What We Offer

Tailored Care for
Every Dog

From energetic beach romps to gentle evening strolls, we offer a range of services designed around your dog's individual needs.

Group Beach Walks

Small groups of compatible dogs exploring Sandringham's stunning coastal trails and off-leash beaches. The perfect way for your dog to socialise while getting the exercise they love.

Solo Walks

One-on-one personalised walks for dogs who prefer their own company, or those still building confidence. Full individual attention in your dog's favourite local spots.

Pet Sitting

In-home visits to feed, play and keep your pet company while you're at work or away. Your pet stays comfortable in their own environment with someone who genuinely cares.

Holiday Care

Going away? We'll take care of your furry family member so you can enjoy your trip with complete peace of mind. Daily updates and photos keep you connected.

Our Story

Passionate About Dogs
& Bayside Life

Paws of Bayside was born from a simple love of dogs and a deep appreciation for the incredible walking terrain our coastal suburbs have to offer. We're proud to serve the Bayside community — exploring the beaches, parks and trails that make this part of Melbourne so special.

We treat every dog as our own. Whether you need daily walks while you're at work, reliable care while you're on holiday, or just a trusted friend for your pup — we're here. Small group sizes mean every dog gets genuine attention and care on every outing.
